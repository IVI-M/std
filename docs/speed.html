How Fast Does Death Arrive for Different Countries

We have seen how the strength of a toxin can be measured by the % of reports that are severe - a stronger toxin produces a greater % of reports that result in death or disability.

We will now look at another measure of the strength of a toxin - namely the speed at which it brings about death or disability. In general, the stronger a toxin, the faster it will kill or maim.

Additional information can be found at Time till Onset

I have chosen 4 countries with the highest % of severe reports - Belgium, Romania, Finland and Sweden. I will compare these with 4 countries of lower % of severe reports - UK, Germany, Austria, France

Here is a table showing the immediacy of death and disability in each of these countries following Covid19 vaccination -



Even though I only looked at a very small sample of countries, there appears to be a trend - namely that those countries reporting the highest % of severe reports, also report the fastest onset of death and disability. Belgium had the highest % of disability reports, and also happens to have the fastest onset of disability - 70% of disabilities began within 48 hours !
